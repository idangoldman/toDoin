ToDoin, app for everyday.

  • New Todo

    "What's Next" field is where you write new todos and submit them by hitting the ENTER key.

  • Edit Todo

    Double click on a todo you want to edit. It will show up in the "What's Next" field in editing mode.

    Hitting the ESC will cancel changes.

  • Complete a Todo

    Click on "V" icon. The icon turned green and the todo crossed? Congrats you have completed a todo!

  • Clear Todos List

    After completing a todo "Eat a X" button will show. Click on it to clear the list. This is still work in progress :)

  • Drag & Drop Todos

    Click on and hold the todo you want, then drag it up or down the list and release the click to drop it.

  • Parsing Links and Email

    Included urls and email addresses into a todo will be parsed into clickble links. Like: blah.co.il and @idan for emails

  • Private Todo

    Click on the glasses and turn them into sunglasses for private todos. Which means they will be blurred out.
