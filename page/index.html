Todoin, app for everyday.

  • New Todo

    "What's Next" strip is where you write new todos and submit them by clicking on the ENTER key.

  • Edit Todo

    Pick a todo you want to edit and double click on it. It will show up in the "What's Next" strip in editing mode.

    Hitting the ESC will cancel changes

  • Sort Todos

    Pick any todo you want click on it and drag it to his new location

  • Complete a Todo

    Complete a todo my clicking on the "check" icon on the right of todo's text. The check icon turned green? congrats you have completed a todo.

  • Private Todo

    If you click on the glasses, they will turn into sunglasses. Which means your todo will be private (blurred out on the screen).

  • Parsing Links and Email addresses

    Seems to be a simple feature which stayed behind for too long. Now all http://blah.co.il and www.blah.co.il links are parse to blah.co.il and idan@blah.co.il email parsed to @idan.

  • Clear Todos from list

    After completing a todo or two a "Eat a X" button will show click on it :)

    This feature got some people confuse, but I still don't want to change it back to "delete completed todos". So any other encouraging activity suggestion to what you want to do after completing a todo will be welcomed.
